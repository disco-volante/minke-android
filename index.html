Welcome to Minke.

Minke is a simple, yet powerful shopping assistant for Android which is maintained here. It also has a web interface which is maintained here. Minke focuses on bringing you accurate and relevant information about products and stores through the use of the ZXing Barcode Scanner. Furthermore it provides additional shopping information such as which is the best store to do shopping for your shopping list and allows you to compare products across stores. Download it here
